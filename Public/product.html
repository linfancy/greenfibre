GARDENING FLOWERPOTS

The recyclable and degradable gardening flowerpots which is made from plate fibre have a strong ability of absorbing water. It is helpful to the water holding capacity of soil and for long time soil moisturizing. In addiction, they are not only beautiful but also practical, dampproof, good light absorbing, strong permeability and no deforming. Its water saving ability is better than the normal plastic and ceramic flowerpots. After abandoning using, they are also the plant fertilizer. All in all, they are the economical and environmental products which meet the circular economy.
