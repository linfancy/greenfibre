Introduction

BRAND INTRODUCTION

Green Fibre represents the lifestyles of health and sustainability. We are not selling products, but engaging in establishing the sentiment between people and people, as well as searching for the most reasonable relation between our environment and lifestyles. It is a brand of natural organic products which includes research and development, design, manufacture and selling. We provide our customers with healthy and eco-friendly products which have concise and quality designs.

OUR PRODUCTS

Our products are derived from the nature, meanwhile, developing in the nature and searching beauty from the nature. In the process of designing and manufacturing, we choose natural organic material for the sake of durability and recycling. We design and create for a kind of healthy lifestyle. So far, Green Fibre products have been exported to Europe, America, Australia, Japan and Korea. The products of Green Fibre have passed the degradation tests and safety tests, such as GB18006, 1-1999&2003，POHS,EN71, EU POHS and FDA.
